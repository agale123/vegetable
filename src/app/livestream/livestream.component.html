Everybody deserves to taste a fresh, ripe, local tomato. At Elk Run Farm, we teach people to grow healthy, natural food for local food banks. That’s how we turn cycles of hunger into profound local empowerment. This spring, we’re live-streaming events that connect you with our healthy farm community. Watch our weekly on-farm videos to learn how we’ve transformed an old golf course into a fertile farm producing healthy food for food banks.

Join us every Wednesday at 1:00 pm from May 13th through June 3rd. Each week, staff and volunteers will demonstrate one of the important stages of growing food for our community.
